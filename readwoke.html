What IS Read Woke?


Read Woke is a book club dedicated to projecting the voice of the oftentimes unheard and challenging the status quo for the betterment of all through reading and discussion.

Each school year is broken up into four quarters, and each quarter we read a different book on a theme that explores a different facet of the human experience.

We'd love for you to join us!
